Wardrobe Design

This project is a custom-designed wardrobe created to optimize storage while maintaining a clean and modern aesthetic. The design focuses on vertical space utilization, allowing maximum storage capacity without overcrowding the room.

The wardrobe features a wood-finish surface that adds warmth to the space, along with a full-length mirror integrated into the door for both functionality and visual depth. The layout ensures easy accessibility and organized storage for daily use.

Positioned near natural light, the design enhances the overall room atmosphere while keeping the structure minimal and practical. The combination of material, proportion, and placement reflects a balance between usability and visual appeal.

  • Space-efficient vertical storage design
  • Integrated full-length mirror
  • Warm wood finish for a modern look
  • Functional layout for everyday usability
